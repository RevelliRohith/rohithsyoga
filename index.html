About Me

Revelli Rohith is a professional Yoga Coach, National Yogasana Medalist and Taekwondo Black Belt with a deep passion for teaching yoga and traditional physical training systems.

He is a Certified Yoga Coach from Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports (NSNIS), Patiala and is currently pursuing a PG Diploma in Yoga.

He is a National Yogasana Judge under Yogasana Bharat and Technical Committee Member of Telangana Yogasana Sports Association.
